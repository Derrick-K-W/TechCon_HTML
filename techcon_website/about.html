History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences worldwide. Over the years, the event has hosted cutting-edge discussions on the latest innovations in AI, cybersecurity, and software development.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education within the tech community. We aim to create a platform where industry leaders, researchers, and tech professionals can come together to share ideas and shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Elijah Smithson

Dr. Elijah Smithson is a pioneer in artificial intelligence and has been a keynote speaker at TechCon for several years. Her research on AI ethics and machine learning has revolutionized the field.

Sir Henry Artikson

Sir Henry Artikson is a cybersecurity expert who has contributed significantly to data protection strategies worldwide. His talk on cybersecurity risks in emerging technologies at TechCon 2021 was highly influential.
